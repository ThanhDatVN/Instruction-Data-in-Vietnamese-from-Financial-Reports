1/2024 cao hơn quý 1/2025 là do khoản lợi nhuận đến từ thu nhập khác.
Net profit after corporate income tax for the first quarter of 2025 was VND
4,128,605,593, a decrease of VND 1,971,631,697 or 32.32% compared to the same
period in 2024. The decline was mainly due to general economic difficulties, which
affected people's spending on healthcare, leading to a decrease in sales compared to the
first quarter of 2024. Net profit from operating activities in Ql/2025 changed
insignificantly compared to Qua ter 1 of2024; however, the higher profit in Quater 1 of
2024 was attributable to other income.
